Oppo F7

The Oppo F7, released in April 2018, is an upper mid-range smartphone that offers a blend of performance and style. Here we will share Oppo F7 price in Bangladesh and features. It features a 6.23-inch IPS LCD display with a resolution of 1080x2280 pixels, protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 5. The device is powered by the MediaTek MT6771 Helio P60 chipset, with 4GB of RAM and 64GB of internal storage, expandable via a dedicated microSDXC slot. For photography offers a 16MP rear camera with features like LED flash, HDR, and panorama. For selfie 25MP front camera. Both cameras are capable of recording 1080p videos. The Oppo F7 runs on Android 8.1 (Oreo) with ColorOS 5. The device includes a glass front, plastic back, and frame, with a rear-mounted fingerprint sensor for security. The device is equipped with a non-removable Li-Ion 3400mAh battery.
